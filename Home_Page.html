Welcome to the Country Catalogue! Here you can explore information about countries around the world.

The world covers 510 million km², with 71% oceans and 29% land, creating a planet dominated by water and shaped by powerful systems like plate tectonics, ocean currents, and atmospheric circulation. Home to over 8 billion people across 7 continents and about 195 countries, Earth contains extraordinary extremes—from the Himalayas (the tallest mountains) to the Mariana Trench (the deepest point), and from the Sahara Desert to the Amazon Rainforest, the largest tropical forest. Its climates range from polar ice caps to equatorial heat, supporting millions of species and some of the richest biodiversity known. Human civilization spans thousands of cultures, languages, and belief systems, driving global networks of trade, technology, and communication that make the modern world deeply interconnected yet incredibly diverse.
